Commercial Services

Industry Leading Expertise & Consistent Customer Satisfaction

The services we offer to our commercial clients include

We provide several different programs within our lawn and bed maintenance service. Keeping garden beds looking pristine all year round requires more than occasional maintenance and putting a few potted plants in the soil. Our team of world-class lawn and landscaping service experts provides the intensive care necessary to keep your commercial property looking excellent through all the seasons. Contact Xtreme Gardens to discuss our services in detail or to schedule an appointment.

 Commercial Lawn Care and Landscaping in Broward and Miami

Leave the health of your property to us, year-round. Tapered to the climate conditions and risks of the area, we provide scheduled, science-driven treatments in every season.

  •  Organic and Traditional Fertilization
  •  Tree and Shrub Care

 Get the Landscape Your Business Deserves

Landscapes are valuable, regardless of the nature of your business… but we understand that the purpose may vary. Whether you’re a restaurant hoping to welcome customers, or an employer seeking to improve employee productivity and communicate value to visitors, we can help. We offer consultations, unique designs, and installations for your commercial landscaping needs.

Every commercial lawn and landscape maintenance plan we offer is personalized. We’re fully equipped with the manpower, advanced techniques and equipment to execute:

  •  Traditional lawn maintenance, including mowing, trimming, and hedging
  •  Shrub/ornamental pruning and detailing
  •  Tree pruning and detailing

 Sod and Mulch Installation

We will send a professional estimator to your home or commercial property free of charge. Our estimator measures the square footage of the area that needs new SOD or Mulch. We give you an estimate in writing or via email with no hidden fees. On the scheduled delivery and installation day our supervisor will oversee the crew and oversee the SOD installation job start to finish.

 Timely Updates and Open Communication

You communicate your goals to us, and we create a planned approach. This ensures every corner of your property, and all of its elements, are taken care of throughout the year. You’ll receive timely updates and open communication to stay informed of your property’s care and progress.
